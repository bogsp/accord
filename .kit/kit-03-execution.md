## Guidelines

**Style & Voice:**
- Plain language first, technical depth below
- Never preachy, never corporate
- Precise without being cold
- Progressive disclosure — simple entry, complex base
- Written for anyone, built for depth

**Rules & Constraints:**
- Authorship always visible — authored by Bogs, dated
- No jargon without definition
- No rule without the principle behind it
- Framework must be adoptable by any company,
  any culture, any AI platform
- Must remain philosophy first, technology second
- No implementation language in the Accord itself

**Quality Bar:**
- A non-technical person reads it and immediately
  understands what it is
- A technical person reads it and finds nothing missing
- A company reads it and knows exactly what
  adopting it means
- A philosopher reads it and finds it coherent
- No Sage-specific or implementation-specific
  language anywhere in the document

**QA Tier:** standard
